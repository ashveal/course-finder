Bachelor of Outdoor and Environmental Education

Summary

Looking for a career in the outdoors, working with young people and exploring the natural environment? The three year Bachelor of Outdoor and Environmental Education is for you.

Career Opportunities

Positions with outdoor education companies, centres and camps. Occupations in recreation (e.g. community organisations, resorts, with municipal councils and tertiary institutions. Tourism (e.g. adventure tourism, ecotourism). Students may go on to study a Masters of Teaching to become qualified teachers
